CID 104 NICIP SNOMED Imaging Procedure

Resources:

None

Type:

Non-extensible

Version:

Not versioned

UID:

1.2.840.10008.6.1.1438

Context Group ID 104 comprises the SNOMED CT Concept-ID codes of the UK National Health Service National Interim Codes for Imaging Procedures.

Coding Scheme Designator is specified as "SCT".

Note

The NICIP SNOMED Subset of Imaging Procedures is not included in the licensing agreement described in Section 2.19 between NEMA and SNOMED International that allows the use within DICOM conformant applications without further license or payment of fee.
