CID 5001 Country

Context Group ID 5001 comprises the two letter country code scheme of ISO 3166. The Coding Scheme Designator (0008,0102) shall be ISO3166_1.

Note

The two letter country codes of ISO 3166 may be obtained at http://www.iso.org/obp/ui/#search/code/
