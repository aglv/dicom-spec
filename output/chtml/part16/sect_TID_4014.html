TID 4014 CAD Image Quality

This Template provides the detail specific to image quality. It allows the encoding of descriptors of image quality (e.g., CID 6041 “Mammography Image Quality Finding”) for a given image or region of an image. For instance, images with partial motion blur can be identified with the region noted.
