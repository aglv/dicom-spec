CID 3046 Sleep Stage

This Context Group comprises codes to classify sleep stages. MDC codes come from table “EEG Annotations – Neurophysiologic Enumeration” of ISO/IEEE 11073-10101. MDC terms included in the table below may not constitute the complete list; see the ISO/IEEE Standard.
