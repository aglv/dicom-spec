CID 3036 EMG Annotation – Neurophysiological Enumeration

This Context Group comprises the nomenclature and codes neurophysiologic enumerations of ISO/IEEE 11073-10101, which apply to electromyography. The terms included in the table below may not constitute the complete list; see the ISO/IEEE Standard.
