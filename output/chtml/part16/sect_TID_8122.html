TID 8122 Animal Feeding

This template encodes a description of feeding and watering of animals, over an interval during which conditions are relatively homogenous.

Note

  1. No specific time interval during which the diet is applicable is described.

  2. Values for product names and codes are expected to be accurate at the time the information is recorded, recognizing that products may evolve over time.
