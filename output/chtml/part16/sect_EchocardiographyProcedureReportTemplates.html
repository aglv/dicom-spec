Echocardiography Procedure Report Templates 

TID 5200 Echocardiography Procedure Report

This Template forms the top of a Content Tree that allows an ultrasound device to describe the results of an adult echocardiography imaging procedure. It is instantiated at the root node. It can also be included in other Templates that need to incorporate echocardiography findings into another report as quoted evidence.

Type:

Extensible

Order:

Significant

Root:
