Note

The concepts used in this context group are more general than those for a specific procedure, e.g., surgery, radiotherapy, chemotherapy. In SNOMED, the concepts used in this context group are the parent concepts of the surgically-specific concepts used in CID 631 “Procedure Phase Requiring Anesthesia”. There are no concepts defined specifically for periods related to an imaging procedure so the general concepts suffice (in context).
