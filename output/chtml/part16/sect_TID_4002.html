TID 4002 Mammography CAD Impression/Recommendation Body

The details of an impression and recommendation are expressed in this Template. It is applied to both Mammography CAD Overall Impression/Recommendation (TID 4001 “Mammography CAD Overall Impression/Recommendation”) and Mammography CAD Individual Impression/Recommendation (TID 4003 “Mammography CAD Individual Impression/Recommendation”).
