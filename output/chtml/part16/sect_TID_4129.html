TID 4129 Colon CAD Geometry

All geometry Template invocations require specification of either the location of the center of the object, the outline, or both. Geometry is a property of single image findings (see TID 4127 “Colon CAD Single Image Finding”) and composite features (see TID 4125 “Colon CAD Composite Feature”).
