TID 4306 PI-RADS® Localized Abnormality Assessment

This template stores PI-RADS® assessment for all analyzed modalities, with the references to the annotations in the individual modalities. Assessment is not specific to lesions, since it can also be used to assess additional findings, including definitely benign findings.

When lesions are identified, only one lesion shall be designated as "Index lesion".

Extensible

Order:
