Content Item Descriptions

Row 1

Specifies the anatomic context of the observations in the group.

Row 2

Details the anatomical location, e.g., proximal, middle, or distal

Row 3

The particular vessel branch, such as the inferior, medial or lateral

Row 5

Cardiac phase (systolic, diastolic), especially for aorta measurements

Row 6

Eating phase, especially for mesenteric and celiac measurements
