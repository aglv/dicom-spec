Note

The electrode names (T3 vs. T7) designate exactly the same position on the head; the difference in naming is due to historical iterations of the nomenclature. T3, T4, T5 and T6 derive from the initial 10-20 electrode naming system, the others from the later 10-10 electrode naming system. Since the electrode names designate the exact same position, they should be considered synonyms and could be used interchangeably. Typically, T7, T8, P7 and P8 are used.
