CID 12322 Elastography Measurement Site

This Context Group contains codes to identify the anatomical location of a specific elastography measurement, such as a specific segment within the liver. Codes for the organ/tissue that is the subject of the elastography study are contained in CID 12321 “Elastography Site”.
