Content Item Descriptions

Row 5b

The laterality of the finding may be different from the laterality of the procedure specified in Row 3, if the latter is undefined or bilateral. If this Content Item is absent, the laterality of the procedure is assumed to apply. The laterality of the finding may be bilateral.
