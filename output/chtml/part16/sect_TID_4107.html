TID 4107 Chest CAD Geometry

All geometry Template invocations require specification of either the location of the center of the object, the outline, or both. Geometry is a property of single image findings (see TID 4104 “Chest CAD Single Image Finding”) and composite features (see TID 4103 “Chest CAD Composite Feature Body”).
