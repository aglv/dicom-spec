CID 1000 CT Transverse Plane Reference Basis

The items in this Context Group provide the basis for defining transverse planes associated with the limits of CT acquisitions and reconstructions. It includes body structures, morphologic abnormalities and physical objects that may be the subject or serve as points of reference for imaging.
