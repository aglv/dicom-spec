TID 10046 Filtration

This template describes the filtration applied to a radiation beam after it has exited the X-Ray tube. It does not include inherent filtration associated with the X-Ray tube itself.

Filters move with the associated X-Ray source (within an X-Ray tube). Attenuating material that does not move with an X-Ray source is referred to as an Attenuator and is described in TID 10047.
