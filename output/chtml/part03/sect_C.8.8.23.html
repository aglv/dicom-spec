Note

C.8.8.23.1 Current Treatment Status

Enumerated Values:

NOT_STARTED

Patient has not yet begun treatment

ON_TREATMENT

Patient is currently undergoing treatment

ON_BREAK

Patient is currently not undergoing treatment, but a resumption date is known

SUSPENDED

Patient is currently not undergoing treatment, but resumption of treatment is planned at an unknown date

STOPPED

Patient has stopped treatment without completing the planned course

COMPLETED

Patient completed the planned course of treatment

A change in the Current Treatment Status (or any other field) in a RT Treatment Summary Record Object shall define a new Instance of the RT Treatment Summary Record IOD.
