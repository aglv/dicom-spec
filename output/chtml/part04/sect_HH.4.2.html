HH.4.2 C-MOVE Operation

See the C-MOVE Operation definition for the Section C.4.2 “C-MOVE Operation”. No Extended Behavior or Relational-Retrieve is defined for the Defined Procedure Protocol Query/Retrieve Service Classes.

Query/Retrieve Level (0008,0052) is not relevant to the Defined Procedure Protocol Query/Retrieve Service Classes, and therefore shall not be present in the Identifier. The only Unique Key Attribute of the Identifier shall be SOP Instance UID (0008,0018). The SCU shall supply one UID or a list of UIDs.

Note

More than one entity may be retrieved, using List of UID matching.
