GG.5.2 SCP Conformance Requirements

An implementation that conforms to a SOP Class of the Non-Patient Object Storage Service as an SCP shall state in its Conformance Statement:

  • The behavior of the SCP in the case of a successful C-STORE operation, including the access method for a stored SOP Instance, and the duration of the storage.

  • The meaning of each case of a failure C-STORE response status, as well as appropriate recovery action.

Note

There may be SOP Class specific Conformance Statement requirements for applications that interpret the SOP Instances for display or further processing. See Section GG.6.
