O.3 Modification of SR Document Content

A device that is an SR Storage SOP Class SCU may modify information in a SOP Instance that it has previously sent or received. When this SOP Instance is modified and sent to an SCP, it shall be assigned a new SOP Instance UID if any of the following conditions are met:

  • addition, removal or update of any Attribute within the SR Document General Module or SR Document Content Module;

  • modification of the Series Instance UID (0020,000E);

  • modification of the Study Instance UID (0020,000D).
