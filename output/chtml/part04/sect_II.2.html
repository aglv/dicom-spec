II.2 Protocol Approval Information Models Definitions

The Protocol Approval Information Models are identified by the SOP Class negotiated at Association establishment time. Each SOP Class is composed of both an Information Model and a DIMSE-C Service Group.

The Protocol Approval Information Models are defined in Section II.6, with the Entity-Relationship Model Definition and Key Attributes Definition analogous to those defined in the Worklist Information Model Definition of the Basic Worklist Management Service.
